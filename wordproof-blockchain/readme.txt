== Description ==

### WordProof: Timestamp your WordPress Content on the Blockchain

> “In five years from now, if you don’t timestamp your articles on the blockchain, you’re going to be considered a fraud.” - Brendan Blumer

With WordProof, you can sign your WordPress content in any EOSIO blockchain from the comfort of your WordPress Dashboard. This works without any middlemen and will be free forever.

== Installation ==

=== From within WordPress ===

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
1. Search for 'WordProof'
1. Activate WordProof Blockchain from your Plugins page.
1. Go to "after activation" below.

=== Manually ===

1. Upload the `wordproof-blockchain` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the WordProof Blockchain plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to "after activation" below.

=== After activation ===

1. Choose which EOSIO chain to connect to. If you don’t have one, create one for free by downloading the [Telos SQRL wallet](https://github.com/Telos-Foundation/Sqrl) (this is a temporary fix, as free account creation will be integrated in the WordProof plugin soon).
1. On every Page and Post update, you’ll be able to sign your submission on any of the EOSIO blockchain.

== Screenshots ==

1. Sign your Post or Page from the sidebar of your editor.
2. Your EOSIO Blockchain Wallet pops up, choose with which account you want to sign your content.
3. Sign your WordPress content with your chosen EOSIO account.
4. If the content is timestamped, a link to the certificate of authenticity appears in the front-end.
5. The certificate with proof of authenticity, a link to the Blockchain is attached.
6. You can choose several popular EOSIO Blockchains. In the next version you'll be able to create your free EOSIO account on Telos.
